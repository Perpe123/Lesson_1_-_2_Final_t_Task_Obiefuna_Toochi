About Campeonas '22

This is the 2022 graduating class of Faculty of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University, Anambra state.

We are 143 students in number comprising of 65 male and 78 female students.

Final year week

This is the week where the Campeonas, just like other university leavers organize and engage in socio-cultural activities.

Here, we had dinner party, picnic, movie night, jeans carnival and so many other fun-filled activities just create and have beautiful memories together after 5 years of stressful academic workload.

The challenges we encountered during this course of study

It was never easy from the very first beginning. It was after some of us were stepped down and had to repeat their 100 level because they didn't pass all the courses offered in the level that we got a clearer understanding of what we signed up for. We had to try the much we could to manage the heavy academic work load; the individual courses with their multiple lecturers each with their own hand outs and study materials, the practical classes some of which we had to work with animals such as rats and toads. Funnily enough, sometimes we had to stay in school till late hours because we had to wait for our little friends (rats) to wake up so we could record how long they were unconscious after inducing anaesthesia or for any other reasons.

The times when most of us get promoted to the next level while some were made to repeat and a few others told to change to another department. The demotion and separation didn't go down well with us emotionally and financially, but then they all happened for the right reasons.

During our final year days it was a different ball game, we all had to multi-task as we did our project, went for clerkship and still attended lectures. We passed through both physical and mental stress just to bag this degree and thank God today we are CAMPEONAS!
